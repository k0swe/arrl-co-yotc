Welcome to Year of the Club

Supporting amateur radio club activities throughout the ARRL Colorado Section

This application is designed to support the American Radio Relay League (ARRL) Colorado Section's Year of the Club activities. Our mission is to promote and facilitate amateur radio club activities throughout Colorado.

About Year of the Club

The Year of the Club initiative encourages amateur radio operators to participate in club activities, contests, and events. This platform helps clubs track their participation and achievements throughout the year.

Features

  • Track club activities and participation
  • View contest results and standings
  • Connect with other Colorado amateur radio clubs
  • Stay updated on ARRL Colorado Section events

Getting Started

  • Create an account (top right)
  • Set name and callsign
  • Go to Clubs
  • Find your club(s) and select "I'm a Member"
  • Wait for approval
  • After approval, create events to use your club callsign and RSVP to others' events
  • After your events, upload your ADIF logs (coming soon)
